Spotlight: NEIU College of Arts and Sciences

The College of Arts and Sciences, led by Dean Wamucii Njogu, is home to more than thirty different departments and programs ranging from Anthropology to Music to Physics. We are happy to serve every student at the university. This term we eagerly anticipate the U.S.-Japan Human Security Symposium on Thursday-Friday, March 29-30; and the 20th Annual NEIU Student Research and Creative Activities Symposium on Friday, April 13.

To keep abreast of exciting opportunities and events in the College, please “like” our NEIU College of Arts & Sciences Fan Page at http://www.facebook.com/#!/pages/NEIU-College-of-Arts-and-Sciences/134744126558362.

To contact department chairs and/or faculty members of the College of Arts and Sciences, please consult this page on our website:  http://www.neiu.edu/Academics/College%20of%20Arts_Sciences/Faculty%20Contact%20Information/Faculty_Contact_Information.html.  The faculty, staff and administrators of the College of Arts and Sciences are deeply committed to helping students succeed.  Please stop by to visit us in BBH 158.
