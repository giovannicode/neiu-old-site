Student Affairs E-Newsletter

February 2012

Featured in this Issue

  • Help us Name the E-Newsletter

    • We welcome suggestions for naming this newsletter from Division of Student Affairs staff. Have an idea for a catchy title? Win a prize if your newsletter name is chosen!

  • First Year Co-Curricular Menu – Updated and Online

    • The First Year Co-Curricular Menu is a resource that lists activities and programs (i.e., clubs, cultural events, support services). The online menu includes a calendar of events that will be continually updated throughout the semester.

  • Introducing the Professional Development Committee

    • The PDC is working on prioritizing its initiatives, including planning future webinars and workshops and administering a survey of division staff on the types of programs and services you need to support your growth and development.

  • Spotlight: College of Arts and Sciences

    • Article highlighting the College of Arts and Sciences, which comprises over thirty departments and programs. Includes a link to contact information for faculty and department chairs.

  • Internal Communications Survey Results
    •

    A summary of the findings of the internal communications survey, which will shape how we communicate with staff in Student Affairs and what type of information we provide.

  • New Targeted Announcements Procedures

    • In response to your feedback, several modifications have been made to streamline the Targeted Announcement process for the division. This article highlights the changes and provides links to the guidelines and form.

  • Mark Your Calendar

    • A list of upcoming events of interest to Student Affairs division staff. Includes on and off campus professional development opportunities, workshops, and more.
