Celebrate the Life and Legacy of Dr. Michael T. Kelly
Thursday, February 16, 2012
3:05 pm - 4:05 pm
Alumni Hall

The staff in the Division of Student Affairs invites you to celebrate the life and legacy of Dr. Michael T. Kelly, past Dean of Students at NEIU. Dean Kelly leaves a legacy of dedication to our student body and the entire NEIU community. He has impacted many lives and encouraged and supported many students throughout their journey in academia. We look forward to the NEIU community coming together to share those fond memories and participate in activities to honor his life and legacy.

Dr. Kelly was very proud of his Irish heritage. In addition to his love for family, friends, and students, he loved all things Irish! We ask that you honor his Irish heritage by wearing green this Thursday.

Event Coordinators: The Dean of Students staff, Student Government Association, and The Office of Student Leadership Development.

Webcast: Proactive Advising to Retain First-Year and Transfer Students
February 22
12:00 - 1:45 pm
SU 217

Retain more first-year and transfer students using an innovative advising model.
Approximately one-third of undergraduate students leave college after their first year. Students who are more actively engaged in college life and have a strong connection to a faculty or staff member are more likely to stay in school and succeed academically.
Join us for this online webcast to learn how you can utilize an innovative advising model at your institution to help retain first-year and transfer students. This program is ideal for administrators and "front-line" staff in academic advising roles that serve first-year and transfer student populations. Please feel free to bring your lunch!

If you are interested in attending, please RSVP to Linda Leyva in Student Affairs at lf-leyva@neiu.edu.
