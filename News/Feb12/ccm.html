First Year Co-Curricular Menu -- Updated and Online!

Getting involved in co-curricular activities outside the classroom can improve students’ opportunities for success both in and out of the classroom and enhance their overall NEIU experience. The First Year Co-Curricular Menu lists activities and programs including student clubs, student leadership opportunities, cultural events, and support services.  The new updated menu also includes a calendar of events for the current semester.

The menu was developed to serve as a resource for FYE Faculty and students to connect them with activities and programs that are meaningful and help them to understand how learning occurs both in and out of the classroom.  As you will see when you review it, the menu is organized into categories/sections that link the activities to the overall goals of the FYE Program.

These sections include:
  • Transitions: Research has shown that students who take part in extracurricular events at a university tend to develop a sense of connectedness and desire to persist at that institution.
  • Self Discovery and Learning Styles
  • Time Management: Budgeting of time for academic success, finding a balance between study, work, and relaxation.
  • Critical Thinking and Inquiry
  • Readiness and Skills for Academic Success

The following are examples of some items that are currently included in the menu: art gallery displays, meeting with a staff member to explore career options, student organization fair, tutoring through the Learning Center, and participating in student government.

The online version of this menu is: http://www.neiu.edu/~stulife/ccMenu.html. This online version will be updated on a regular basis. Any NEIU staff can submit items of interest to first year students for consideration for inclusion in the Co-Curricular Menu. Please contact Kerri Kadow at k-kadow@neiu.edu for more information.
