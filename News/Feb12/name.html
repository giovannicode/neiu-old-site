Help us Name the E-Newsletter!

Welcome to the inaugural edition of the Student Affairs E-Newsletter. We hope you find the information and articles inside useful. If you would like to submit an idea, event, or write an article for the newsletter, please email Kerri Kadow at k-kadow@neiu.edu. We welcome any and all suggestions and contributions. This is your newsletter. In that spirit, we would like to get suggestions for naming this newsletter from you, the employees of the Division of Student Affairs.

Do you have an idea for a catchy title? Let us know by sending an email to Kerri Kadow at k-kadow@neiu.edu with the subject line “Newsletter Name Suggestion”. All suggestions will be considered by the Leadership Team and the person with the winning name will receive a prize. We look forward to seeing all your great ideas!
