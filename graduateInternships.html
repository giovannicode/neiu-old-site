Graduate Internships

An internship in the Division of Student Affairs at Northeastern Illinois University (NEIU) provides graduate students an opportunity to gain experience at the only public four-year university in the Midwest federally designated as a Hispanic Serving Institution.

Northeastern Illinois University is a fully accredited public university serving the Chicago metropolitan area.
