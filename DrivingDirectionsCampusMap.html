Driving Directions, Public Transportation, and Campus Map

Driving Directions to Campus

The campus is in close proximity to main roads, expressways, and rapid transit. The main roads on the north and south of campus are Bryn Mawr (5600 North) and Foster (5200 North). Main east and west roads are St. Louis (3500 West) and Pulaski (4000 West).

The main parking entrance to the campus is on Central Park (3600 West). Guest parking is available in university surface parking lots D, E, F, H, J, or L and on the 2nd floor or higher in the parking garage. Daily guest parking permits for these areas are dispensed from the Daily Parking Permit Kiosks (print map in Landscape mode for the best results) located at the south end of parking lot H and on the first floor of the parking garage. The cost is $5.00 and the kiosks accept coins or bills.

From O'HARE INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT, take the Kennedy Expressway (I-90) to the Austin-Foster exit; proceed east on Foster to Pulaski, proceed north (left turn) on Pulaski to Bryn Mawr, and then east (right turn) on Bryn Mawr to the parking lot entrance and turn right into the parking lots.

From DOWNTOWN CHICAGO, take the Kennedy (I-90 / 94) north, merge with the Edens Expressway (I-94) and proceed north; exit at Peterson; proceed east on Peterson to Pulaski, proceed south (right turn) on Pulaski to Bryn Mawr, and then east (left turn) on Bryn Mawr to the parking lot entrance and turn right into the parking lots.

From the East, take Lake Shore Drive south or north, exit at Foster and proceed west to Central Park. Proceed north (right turn) into the parking lots.
