Welcome to Student Affairs!

NEIU is your University and we are so glad you are here. It is a great place to learn and to achieve your academic and personal goals. At NEIU, we are committed to your success!

Our students lead busy lives—with classes, homework, University activities, family responsibilities, work, and community involvement. Please take advantage of student services and programs at Northeastern that can answer questions, solve problems, and help you complete your degree in a timely manner.

The Division of Student Affairs connects you to people and programs that are here to help you be successful. Have a great experience at Northeastern—where our students learn in the city and lead in the world.

To all our new and returning Golden Eagles, best wishes for a successful school year!

Frank E. Ross, Ph.D.
Vice President for Student Affairs

Important Info

Here is some important information for current students:

  • You can check your financial aid status via NEIUport by visiting the ‘Current Student’ tab and clicking ‘Financial Aid Awards’.
  • If you have a question about your financial aid award, please call Financial Aid at (773) 442-5016.
  • For questions about your bill and/or payments, please call the Cashier at (773) 442-5184.
  • Please visit the ‘What’s New?’ section of the NEIU home page to find out the latest news about NEIU.
  • You are strongly encouraged to sign up for the University's emergency communication system, N ALERT to receive text messages, voice mails and/or e-mails notifying you of emergencies including extreme weather-related situations. Signing up is quick and easy; just follow these steps:
    1. Log in to NEIUport at neiuport.neiu.edu
    2. Click on "Student" tab and employees click on "Employee" tab
    3. In the left column under "Personal Information" click on "Update address and phone/
      N Alert" and "Update E-mailAddresses/N Alert"
