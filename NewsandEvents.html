NEIU Receives AAC&U Grant

February 28, 2013 - NEIU received a grant from the Bringing Theory to Practice Project of the Association of American Colleges and Universities (AAC&U) to enhance our work in civic engagement. Kris Pierre, Assistant to the Vice President for Student Affairs, provided leadership for development of this grant proposal.
read more

NEIU's Assistant Vice President for Student Affairs Receives National Award

January 25, 2013 - NEIU's Assistant Vice President for Student Affairs Juan R. Guardia is the 2013 NASPA Mid-Level Student Affairs Professional Award Recipient. This award recognizes individuals who demonstrate outstanding commitment to the profession and have contributed to programs that address the needs of students.
read more

Northeastern Illinois University's VP for Student Affairs Chairs Conference

January 18, 2013 - The 2013 NASPA Annual Conference will be held March 16-20 in Orlando and Northeastern Illinois University's (NEIU) Vice President for Student Affairs Frank Ross is serving as the 2013 Program Committee Chair.
