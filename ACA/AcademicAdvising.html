Academic Advising

Welcome!

The Academic Advising promotes student success by providing students with the information, guidance and support they need to make informed decisions. In this way, the Academic Advising encourages students to take responsibility for achieving their academic goals, thereby empowering students for the road to lifelong learning.

Throughout the advising process students are provided with opportunities to:

-Learn about educational options, degree requirements, and university policies

-Clarify educational, career and life goals through the development of a meaningful academic plan

-Become familiar with support services and resources

The Academic Advising is located on the lower level of Lech Walesa Hall, west side, in Room LWH-0027. Staff advisors at the Center are available on a walk-in basis:

Monday - Thursday 8:00 A.M. - 3:15 P.M.
Friday 8:00 A.M. - 1:00 P.M.

Advisors are also available evenings by appointment: Monday through Thursday between 4:00 pm and 6:30 pm.

To schedule an evening appointment please call (773) 442-5470

PLEASE NOTE: During peak registration periods throughout the year the office gets busy and wait times may be lengthy, particularly between 12:00pm and 3:00pm. Please try to plan accordingly.

Fax Number (773) 442 - 5434
