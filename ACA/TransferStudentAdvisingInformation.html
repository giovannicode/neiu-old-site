Transfer Student Advising Information

If you are transferring less than 60 credit hours or are undecided as to your major, you should meet with an advisor in the Academic Advisement Center. Advisors are available on a walk-in basis, Monday thru Thursday 8:00 a.m. until 3:15 p.m. and Friday's from 8:00 a.m. till 1:00 p.m.

Evening hours are available by appointment Monday thru Thursday. To schedule an evening appointment, please call (773) 442-5470. The Academic Advisement and Testing Center is located on the lower level of Lech Walesa Hall, west side, in Room
LWH-0027.

If you have earned an A.A. or an A.S. degree and have already decided upon your major, we urge you to meet with an advisor in your intended major department. To contact a major advisor and inquire about hours of availability, please click on the appropriate link below:
