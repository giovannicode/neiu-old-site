Placement & Testing

Testing administers and coordinates a variety of assessment programs and services, including placement testing in English (Writing and Reading) and mathematics and the English Competency Examination (ECE).

Testing is located on the lower level of Lech Walesa Hall, west side, in Room LWH-0027.

TEST REGISTRATION

Registration for all placement tests can be done in person at Testing, located in Lech Walesa Hall, LWH 0027 or by phone at 773-442-5293. Refer to the current test schedule. Any cancellations or changes must be made 48 hours in advance. Absence from an exam without proper cancellation will prevent further registration; a student who fails to appear 3 times for his/her test will not be eligible for future registration of the test but will have to rely on walk-in availability.

TEST SCHEDULE

Students must register in person or by phone before taking the test.

TEST START TIMES

Please note that placement tests are typically scheduled back to back on the same day beginning first with the Math Placement Test followed by the writing portion of the English Placement Test and finally, the reading portion of the English Placement Test. 

If you are not scheduled to take the Math Placement Test,  please be aware that the writing portion of the English Placement Test begins 1 hour and 20 minutes after the start time indicated on the test schedule, and the reading portion begins 2 hours and 20 minutes after the time indicated on the schedule. Click here for sample start times.

PLEASE NOTE: During peak registration periods throughout the year the office gets busy and wait times may be lengthy, particularly between 12:00pm and 3:00pm. Please try to plan accordingly.
