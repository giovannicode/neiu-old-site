New Student & Family Programs

We are excited to announce the upcoming addition of this new office to the Division of Student Affairs:

New Student and Family Programs will be open for business to serve our students and university community for the 2012-2013 Academic Year.

The New Student & Family Programs office is designed to focus on the successful transition of entering freshmen and transfer students. This office will coordinate student orientation, the university placement and testing services, summer transition program, minority student mentoring program, peer leaders, and family and parent programming.
