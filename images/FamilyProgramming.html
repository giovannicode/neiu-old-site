Family Programming

Welcome

Dear Families of NEIU Students,

Welcome to the Family Programming Website!

We at Northeastern Illinois University would like to take this opportunity to welcome you to the extended NEIU family. The primary goals of Family Programming are to increase your knowledge about and involvement in the cultural, recreational, and social activities on campus; and to assist you in gaining a better understanding of the life of the campus. As family members of students at Northeastern, you are an important part of the NEIU community.

We know that the support of family members is vital to student success, especially at commuter institutions like Northeastern. Therefore, we want to offer you this invitation to become engaged in our community. Feel free to visit this web site frequently for information about family-oriented programming at Northeastern. Social networking sites such as Facebook are also being utilized to ensure you are kept aware of what is happening on campus and how to get and stay involved.

In addition, we are continuing to build an e-mail recipient list to inform families of NEIU students about future events. If you would like to have your e-mail address included in the list please enter your email address and click "join" at the top of the sidebar on the right.

A University Family Guide has recently been published which contains useful information about NEIU and helpful tips in supporting your students as they progress toward earning their degrees. Also, the NEIU Foundation will be partnering with us in organizing a Family Association where you will have the ability to comment on how the University can expand and enhance student-focused and family-oriented programs and services.

Our students’ success is, indeed, a goal shared by all of us. Please consider becoming a part of this important effort. We look forward to creating a productive and rewarding relationship with you.

We cannot achieve our commitment to student success without your
involvement and support.

Events

Family Day
April/May
12:00 - 6:30 pm
University Commons

 

The Family Programming Committee of Student Affairs and Northeastern Programming Board (NPB) invite all of NEIU’s students, staff, and faculty to bring their families for NEIU’s Family Day each spring in the University Commons. This is a free event for the entire NEIU community.

The celebration kicks off at 12:00 pm with free food (while supplies last), helium balloons, and carnival games, with many more fun events and activities throughout the day.

The event concludes with a free family-friendly movie screening. Please visit the web site for a full schedule of events: www.neiu.edu/~progbrd/familyday Family Day

Presented by the Family Programming Committee of Student Affairs and Northeastern Programming Board (NPB). For more information, contact Matt Specht (m-specht@neiu.edu, ext. 4184) or NPB (npbneiu@gmail.com, ext. 4590).

Family Orientation
Saturday, July 21, 2012 (8:00 am to 1:00 pm)
Wednesday, August 22, 2012 (5:00 pm to 10:00 pm)

Family Orientation for families of newly admitted students covers an overview of services, university expectations, a tour of our beautiful campus, and much more. The program is designed to assist you, the family, with becoming stress free about your student's academic career and overall success.

Registration will open approximately one month prior to each orientation session. For more information, please contact us at ext. 4610.
