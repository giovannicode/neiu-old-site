Orientation Programs

Northeastern Illinois University and the Office of the Dean of Students congratulate all newly admitted students on their decision to attend NEIU!

We are excited that you have chosen NEIU and hope that your experience is an excellent one. The Office of the Dean of Students, Enrollment Services and Advisement has planned Orientation Programs for you to become acquainted with NEIU and to make your life easier as a First-Year Student, Transfer Student and new Family. We know that college is an exciting and challenging time in life. With this in mind, NEIU Orientation Programs are designed to serve and support your transition and academic success.

First Year Student Orientation

First Year Orientation serves to help new first year students easily adjust to the college experience by getting to know how NEIU best supports their academic and professional success.

The First Year Orientation Program is facilitated by a team of Peer Leaders specifically trained to introduce you to campus life. The First Year Orientation Program will help you become acquainted with NEIU to make life easier as a student. Freshmen must attend Orientation in order to register for classes, meet with an advisor, register for parking, receive your student ID card and learn more about services and resources at NEIU.

The student registration fee for Orientation is $65.

All first year students entering college for the first time must attend the full day orientation from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. to register for courses.

All first year students are required to complete the placement exams prior to attending your Orientation. Please register for orientation and placement exams by contacting the Academic Advising Center at (773) 442-5470. Students enrolled to take Fall courses attend during the Summer and those enrolled to take Spring courses attend orientation in December.

Transfer Student Orientation

Transfer Student Orientation is designed to acquaint new students with Northeastern who have previously earned academic credit.

Transfer Orientations are held at the Main Campus in the Student Union (Golden Eagles Room).

You will learn more about NEIU academic programs, services, and opportunities.Separate transfer orientation tracks are offered to students interested in the College of Arts and Sciences, the College of Business and Management, and the College of Education.

Family Orientation

We welcome families to the NEIU community to gain personal insight into the kind of university experience a student will have at Northeastern Illinois University.

NEIU values "Integrity, Excellence, Access to Opportunity, Diversity, Community, and Empowerment through Learning." The Family Orientation serves for some as a new chapter, for others a welcome opportunity to experience that which only NEIU offers. During this program, families meet, ask questions, and get answers. Family Orientation covers an overview of services, university expectations, a tour of the beautiful campus, and much more. The program is designed to assist you, the family, with becoming stress free about your student's academic career and overall success.
