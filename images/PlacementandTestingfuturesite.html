Placement & Testing

Welcome! University Placement and and Testing administers and coordinates a variety of assessment programs and services, including placement testing in English (Writing and Reading) and mathematics and the English Competency Examination (ECE).

TEST REGISTRATION

Registration for all placement tests can be done in person at the Academic Advisement and Testing Center, located in Lech Walesa Hall, LWH 0027 or by phone at 773-442-5293. Refer to the current test schedule. Any cancellations or changes must be made 48 hours in advance. Absence from an exam without proper cancellation will prevent further registration; a student who fails to appear 3 times for his/her test will not be eligible for future registration of the test but will have to rely on walk-in availability.

TEST SCHEDULE

TEST START TIMES

Please note that placement tests are typically scheduled back to back on the same day beginning first with the Math Placement Test followed by the writing portion of the English Placement Test and finally, the reading portion of the English Placement Test. 

If you are not scheduled to take the Math Placement Test,  please be aware that the writing portion of the English Placement Test begins 1 hour and 20 minutes after the start time indicated on the test schedule, and the reading portion begins 2 hours and 20 minutes after the time indicated on the schedule.
