Understanding Our Students:
Veterans and Parents

November 5, 2010
10:00 am - 12:00 pm
SU 214



The Student Life Professional Development Committee is hosting another workshop in the continuing series Understanding Our Students.  This workshop will focus on students who are veterans and parents and will take place on Friday, November 5, 10:00 am – 12:00 pm.

Light breakfast and beverages will be served 9:30 am – 10:00 am, followed by a student panel and small group discussion.

Please join us in learning more about our student population and how we can better serve them.  If you have specific questions or discussion topics you would like to hear addressed in this workshop, please e-mail them to Megan Krone in Campus Recreation at m-krone@neiu.edu

For more information, please contact Ruth Johnson at r-johnsonb@neiu.edu or ext. 4610.
