About Us

The office of the Vice President for Student Affairs provides administrative support to the departments that comprise the Division of Student Affairs. These departments provide leadership for the co-curriculum that complements and supplements the academic areas, the out-of-classroom education of students, and the diverse services essential to successful retention and graduation.

Our goals reflect Northeastern Illinois University's focus on integrity, excellence, access to opportunity, diversity, community and empowerment through learning, enabling students to develop to their highest potential. Our programs and services enhance the intellectual climate of the University by providing a seamless learning and living experience that recognizes the unique needs of all students.

The Division of Student Affairs has made a commitment to pursue the development of a focused, caring, ethical, and diverse community on the Northeastern Illinois University campus.

Please see Programs and Services for a list of departments in the division.
